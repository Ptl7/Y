Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 6, 2024

At Patel Books, we value your privacy and are committed to ensuring transparency regarding how we handle your information. This Privacy Policy outlines the practices related to data collection, usage, and disclosure, in compliance with global regulations.

1. No Personal Data Collection

Patel Books does not collect personal data such as names, emails, or IP addresses. We do not use analytics or tracking technologies, making your browsing experience completely anonymous.

2. Use of Google Books API

The content shown on Patel Books is fetched from the Google Books API. We do not store or process this data. The responsibility for data accuracy and legality rests with Google LLC or relevant third-party providers.

3. Buy Me a Coffee Integration

Patel Books uses *Buy Me a Coffee* for monetization. When you support us via *Buy Me a Coffee*, your payment and personal data will be processed by that platform under their privacy policy. We recommend reviewing Buy Me a Coffee's Privacy Policy to understand how your information is handled.

4. No Data Sharing

Since we do not collect personal data, there is no data to share with third parties for advertising, marketing, or any other purposes.

5. Security Measures

We implement security measures to protect the website from unauthorized access, although we do not collect personal data on this site.

6. Global Compliance

This Privacy Policy complies with global regulations such as GDPR (EU), CCPA (California), and other privacy laws.
